Mark Rober

Mark Rober is an American youtuber, scientist and engineer. He was born in 1980 and is currently (2022) 41 years old. He initially worked as an engineer for NASA. He worked at NASA for 9 years, out of which, he spent 7 years on the Curosity rover. He later worked in Apple for 4 years. He started his channel in 2015. His Youtube channel took flight when he uploaded a video on a Halloween costume. He has made several appearances in the Jimmy Kimmel Show. He has also collaborated with MrBeast to start fundraising campaigns like #TeamTrees and #TeamSeas. Rober left NASA in June 2013 when he left to pursue "Digital Dudz", a range of Halloween costumes that integrate mobile apps with clothing, which then he sold to Morphsuits, for two years. As of recently, he was presented with an opportunity to return to his Engineering roots to do some ideation type of work for a tech company near San Francisco.
